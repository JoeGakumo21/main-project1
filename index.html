Introduction

Hillsong Worship

Is an Australian praise and worship band that originated from Sydney in the year 1983 at Hillsong Church. The band has been ranked best by the Billboard magazine charts in US, with their best song "What a Beautiful Name" that was presented on 2016 making the band reach platinum stage in US. The band members are Darlene Zschech, Marty Sampson, Brooke Fraser, Reuben Morgan and Taya Smith
